Getting started

FamilyPocket is a pocket money tracker for parents. Add each child, set an allowance, log spending or jobs, and the running balance updates for the whole family. Everything is entered by parents — kids don't need a login, an email address or a card.

Common questions

  • Does FamilyPocket hold real money? No. It only records balances you enter yourself.
  • Do my kids need their own account? No. Parents manage everything from their device.
  • Can both parents use it? Yes — multi-parent sync is part of the app.
  • How do I delete my data? See Delete your account below.

Delete your account and data

You can delete your FamilyPocket account and all associated data at any time from inside the app. Deletion is permanent and removes all children, balances, transactions, jobs, and family data from our servers.

Steps:

  • Open FamilyPocket on your device.
  • Tap the Settings icon (top right of the home screen).
  • Scroll down to the Account section.
  • Tap Delete Account and all data.
  • Follow the confirmation prompts. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

If you have an active subscription, cancel it on the App Store or Google Play before or after deleting — deleting your account does not automatically cancel a subscription.

If you cannot access the app, email support@familypocket.co.nz and we will delete your account manually.

Report a problem

If something isn't working — a bug, a sync issue, or a balance that looks wrong — please email us with a short description and, if you can, a screenshot. We read every message.
