Getting started

FamilyPocket is a pocket money tracker for parents. Add each child, set an allowance, log spending or jobs, and the running balance updates for the whole family. Everything is entered by parents — kids don't need a login, an email address or a card.

Common questions

  • Does FamilyPocket hold real money? No. It only records balances you enter yourself.
  • Do my kids need their own account? No. Parents manage everything from their device.
  • Can both parents use it? Yes — multi-parent sync is part of the app.
  • How do I delete my data? You can clear individual entries in the app, or delete your account to remove everything. Email us if you'd like help.

Report a problem

If something isn't working — a bug, a sync issue, or a balance that looks wrong — please email us with a short description and, if you can, a screenshot. We read every message.
