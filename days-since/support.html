Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your events, resets, and reminders are stored privately on your device (and synced to your own iCloud if you're signed in). We have no server and never see your data.

Does Days Since sync across my devices?

Yes — as long as you're signed into the same iCloud account on each device, your data syncs automatically in the background.

I got a new iPhone. Will my data come across?

Yes. Restore from an iCloud backup or sign into iCloud on your new device and your data will sync over within a few minutes.

What is the free limit?

You can add up to 50 events and reminders combined for free. Days Since Pro removes this limit.

What does Days Since Pro include?

Pro unlocks unlimited events and reminders. It's a one-time purchase — no subscription.

I already bought Pro but it's showing the free limit. How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchase". Your Pro status will be re-applied immediately.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are upcoming dates (birthdays, anniversaries, holidays) that count down to the next occurrence. You can enable local notifications so Days Since reminds you in advance — choose from 1 week, 3 days, 1 day, or same day, at 9 am.

How do I import from Apple Calendar?

Tap the import button in the app, grant calendar access when prompted, and Days Since will suggest recurring events and birthdays from your Apple Calendar to add as events or reminders.

How do I delete an event or reminder?

Swipe left on the item in the main list and tap Delete, or open the detail view and scroll to the bottom.

The day count looks wrong. What's happening?

Day counts reset to 0 at midnight on your device's local time. If you've just crossed midnight, try pulling to refresh. If it still looks off, check that your device's date and time are set automatically in Settings → General → Date & Time.
