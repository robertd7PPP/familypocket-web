Summary

FamilyPocket is a pocket money tracker for families. It records balances, allowance, jobs and wishlist items that parents enter themselves. It is not a bank, payment service, wallet, or stored-value product, and it does not hold real money.

What we collect

  • Account details for the parent who signs up (such as email address).
  • Information parents add about their family: child first names or nicknames, pocket money balances, transactions, allowance schedules, jobs and wishlist items.
  • Basic device and usage information needed to run the app reliably.

What we don't do

  • We don't create accounts or logins for children.
  • We don't collect children's email addresses, phone numbers or contact details.
  • We don't issue debit cards or hold real money on anyone's behalf.
  • We don't sell your data, and we don't show ads.

How we use information

We use the information you provide to operate FamilyPocket: to sync your family's data across your devices, keep balances accurate, and improve the product. We only share data with service providers that help us run the app (for example, hosting and crash reporting), under appropriate confidentiality terms.

Your choices

You can edit or delete the information in your family at any time from within the app, and you can delete your account to remove your data. To request help, contact us at hello@familypocket.app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@familypocket.app.
