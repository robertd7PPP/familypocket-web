The problem

Still tracking pocket money in your head?

Pocket money starts simple. Then someone gets birthday money, spends $7 at the shops, saves for something special, completes a job, forgets what they were owed, and suddenly no one is sure what the balance should be.

FamilyPocket gives your family one clear place to track it — without turning pocket money into kid banking.

How it works

Four steps. No bank account in sight.

01

Add your kids

Create a simple profile for each kid. Use a first name, nickname or label — no kid login required.

02

Track their balance

Add pocket money, birthday cash and rewards. Record spending in seconds. Running total always shown.

03

Show what they're saving for

Add wishlist items so kids see what they have and what they need. Spending towards goals, not just away from them.

04

Upgrade when ready

FamilyPocket Plus adds kid device access, automatic allowance and jobs kids can claim.
