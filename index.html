The everyday problem

Still tracking pocket money in your head?

Pocket money starts simple. Then someone gets birthday money, spends a few dollars at the shops, saves for something special, completes a job, and suddenly no one is quite sure what the balance should be.

FamilyPocket gives your family one clear place to track it — without turning pocket money into kid banking.

FAQ

Pocket money app FAQs

Is FamilyPocket a bank account?

No. FamilyPocket is a pocket money tracker. It does not hold money, issue cards or move funds.

Does FamilyPocket hold real money?

No. FamilyPocket does not hold, store, move or manage real money. It records pocket money balances agreed within your family.

Does my kid need an account?

No. Kids do not need separate accounts, emails or sign-ups. Parents can add kids using names, nicknames or labels.

Is there advertising?

No. FamilyPocket is ad-free. It does not show ads to parents or kids.

Can kids use FamilyPocket on their own device?

Yes, with Family Plan. A parent can add another device to the household using a join code.

What is included in the free version?

The free version includes parent-only tracking on one device, unlimited kids, add money, record spending, transaction history and wishlist tracking.

What is included in Family Plan?

Family Plan includes everything in Free, plus kid device access, second parent device access, automatic weekly allowance and jobs kids can claim.
